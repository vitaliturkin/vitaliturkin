About Me.

Hi, I’m a final-year Web Design and Development student at Edinburgh Napier University with a strong focus on front-end development and UX design. I love bringing ideas to life in the browser — creating clean, responsive, and user-friendly web applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and modern UX principles.

My journey into tech started from a genuine interest in problem-solving and understanding how people interact with digital products. Along the way, I’ve also explored Python, TypeScript, Node.js, and Angular, and I’m always curious to try new technologies — especially where UX meets AI.

Outside of coding, I’ve built solid teamwork and leadership skills through collaborative uni projects and past customer-facing roles. I take pride in being organised, adaptable, and someone who gets things done.

Originally from Estonia, I speak Estonian, Russian, and English — which helps me work comfortably in diverse, international teams. I’m currently looking for opportunities in front-end development or digital product design where I can keep learning, growing, and building things that make a real difference.
